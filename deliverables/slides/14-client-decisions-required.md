# Slide 14 — Decisions Required From You

## Slide Title
What we need you to decide so we can start

## Objective
End with a clear, actionable close. Nothing fluffy. Move toward a signed SOW.

## Key Points (on slide)

We need 7 decisions from you to kick off. None are complex.

1. **WhatsApp number** — Keep your current number (we migrate it to API) or use a new number? *Recommendation: keep current, for continuity.*
2. **Data strategy** — Option A (fresh) or Option B (one-time import)? *Recommendation: Option B if you have a reasonably clean list.*
3. **Assignment logic** — Confirm whether we start with fixed-exec-per-customer, area-based, or hybrid. *Recommendation: hybrid — fixed for repeats, area for new.*
4. **Routing pattern** — Group + tags (recommended) or shared web inbox?
5. **Support model** — 45 or 60 days included, then pay-as-you-go. Do you want an optional AMC quoted separately?
6. **Account ownership** — Meta Business, Railway, Supabase, AI provider — all accounts in your business name. Confirm.
7. **Commercial option** — A (₹30k) or B (₹45k)?

## Visual
A checklist on screen, with spaces for initials / ticks next to each decision. Print-friendly — you can literally hand a printout and have the owner tick through it.

## Speaker Notes
"That's the deck. To actually start, I need seven yes-or-no decisions from you. None of them are risky; most have a clear recommended answer already. If we can agree on these in the next few days, I'll send a one-page SOW reflecting exactly what we discussed, you sign it, and we kick off the week after. The fastest path to seeing this live is: agree in principle today, I send SOW tomorrow, you sign, we start Monday. You're looking at a working system by the end of next month."

## Questions to Ask
- Is there anything on this list you are uncomfortable deciding today? Why?
- Is there a decision I haven't asked for that you expected me to?
- Who besides you needs to sign off on this? (partner, CA, spouse — all valid)

## What to Capture
- A soft "yes" or "no" on each of the 7 decisions.
- The names of any other stakeholders involved in the decision.
- Their preferred next step: SOW tomorrow, second meeting first, or time to think.

## Notes for you (not on slide)
- If they're enthusiastic but say "let me get back to you", send the SOW within 24 hours. Momentum is everything.
- If they hesitate on the price, do *not* drop the price on the spot. Instead offer to "reshape the scope" and buy yourself 24 hours to come back with a version that's still honest.
- If they push for "can you do this for 20k?", the answer is: "for 20k, I can do a genuinely lighter version — here's what I'd take out". Never say yes to a 33% discount without removing 33% of scope. See `playbook/05-negotiation-strategy.md`.
